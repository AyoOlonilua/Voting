How to Vote

As the next General Election draws near, it is necessary to let citizens who are 18 years and above know the voting procedure.It is your civic responsibility as a citizen of Nigeria to be involved in electing our leaders.This is what election is all about and there cannot be election without voters.
Before you vote check your Eligibility status if you are deemed eligibly, Register then come back to this at the time appointed for voting to cast your vote.
